Find, Define, Create Value from an International Perspective

As a Brand and Marketing Advisor located in Tokyo, I have had the pleasure of working under and advising Agency Owners and Presidents with a combined experience in the Japanese market of over 70+ years. They include a Former Sony VP, a Former Wunderman Planning Director, and a Former Hakuhodo Creative Director.


I can draw on my cultural background from three different countries to advise you. I grew up in the US, lived in Italy, and have been living in Japan, my father's home country, for two years now. Therefore, I can advise on how to create value from a Japanese, European, and American value perspective.


I look forward to offering my creative and research skills to help you discover and clearly define your current value in the Japanese and global marketplace, and to help you create more value in the future.
