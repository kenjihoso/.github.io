About Me

Hello!

I’m Kenji Hosokawa, a half Japanese and half Italian Account Manager & Researcher in the New York Area who has collaborated with ad agencies (DDB, LEAP, and Hogarth) for over 100 projects with brands such as Nike, Amazon, Bayer, PlayStation, and Cisco.

My international perspective comes from my experience of working globally and my fluency in English, Italian, and Japanese. My global experience and unique background allows me to meet multinational client needs, research complex consumer issues, and manage projects on an international scale.

I look forward to working together to create value from an international perspective.
